A detailed product description

Online retail is often about selling a story, an idea that fits the needs or current whim of your audience. Obviously, online stores do not have a live store attendant (though some may offer live chat), so the sale items need to have a product description that takes the place of your best sales person.

Superior photos and image options

There’s a reason people are always quoting “a picture is worth a thousand words because it is.In the case of internet retail however, it may be worth more. Your product images will influence online shoppers in ways that have them running away or drive them to purchase.

Customer reviews of the product

Last year, BrightLocal conducted its 4th annual study on consumer usage and attitudes on customer reviews. It concluded that 88% of consumers say they trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations, a statistic that was up 9% from the previous year.
